BASICALLY OLD CARS

Classic vehicles might range in age from 10 to 50+ years old, and the term "classic car" may include vintage, antique, and collector vehicles.
Vintage car can be defined as "Many organizations consider vintage cars to be manufactured between 1919 and 1930 (if they even distinguish vintage cars from classics and antiques). The vintage car period generally ends in 1930 with the start of the Great Depression, which greatly impacted the players in the automotive industry". Antique cars are said to be more loosely defined as being at least 25 to 45 years old, depending on the organization. Classic cars are defined as "Even more loosely defined, and still dependent on the organization, classic cars might include antique, vintage, and collector vehicles that are at least 10 to 25 years old".

Why Vintage cars are the best ?

They evoke feelings of nostalgia and romance, and they're something that many people aspire to own. When it comes to classic cars, there's no doubt that vintage vehicles are the ones to beat. No matter what era of car you prefer, you can always find something unique and old-school about a vintage vehicle.
